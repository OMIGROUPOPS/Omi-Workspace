## Answer: dips are LATE-concentrated
- **≥3c dips rise through the window and jump in the final hour**: 38% (B1) → **54% (B4)**.
- **≥10c dips nearly double** by the final hour: 12% (B1) → **23% (B4)**.
- Book coverage also rises toward T-20m (more tickers have an active book closer to match time).
- **Implication for posting:** the deepest, most frequent dips cluster in the **final 60 minutes (T-1h20m → T-20m)** — positioning near match start, not early premarket discovery. Posting bids later in the window (closer to T-20m) catches materially more dip opportunity, at the cost of less time-in-book.
