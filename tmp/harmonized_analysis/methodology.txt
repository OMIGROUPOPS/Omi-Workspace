Ultimate Cell Economics Analysis — Methodology
============================================================

Data Sources:
  1. historical_events: 1357 events, Mar 20 - Apr 17, 2026
  2. DCA v3 results: /tmp/validation5/per_cell_dca_summary.csv
     (515M BBO rows, inline state machine, time-ordered)
  3. Live bot logs: 7 files (last 7 days)
     126 fills, 101 exits, 1364 settlements
  4. deploy_v4.json: 25 active + 18 disabled cells

Cell Classification:
  tier × direction × 5c price bucket
  Entry price = first_price from historical_events
  TWP = fraction of entries in that cell that won settlement
  Mispricing = TWP*100 - avg_entry_price (in cents)

Scalp WR Calculation:
  Scalp = max_price >= entry + exit_cents at any point during match lifecycle
  This includes in-play price movement (exit orders stay resting)

ROI Calculation:
  EV/fill = scalp_rate * exit_c + (1-scalp_rate) * (TWP*(99-entry) - (1-TWP)*(entry-1))
  ROI = EV/fill / entry * 100
  $/fill = EV/fill * 10_contracts / 100_cents_per_dollar

Known Issues:
  - Task 6: Settlement PnL is per-contract not per-position (double-counted)
  - Live log ticker→cell mapping fails for some older entries (shows '?')
  - 32%% of cell matches have blank anchor_source in logs
  - Monte Carlo uses uniform sampling from historical distribution
    (doesn't model serial correlation or regime changes)
