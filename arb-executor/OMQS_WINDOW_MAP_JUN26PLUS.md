## VERDICT - does discount->multi-window, distortion->W2-only, systematically?
DIRECTIONALLY YES, as a gradient (not a clean binary). Mean windows reachable falls monotonically with fill quality: deep-discount 1.23 -> discount 1.02 -> distortion/deep-distortion 0.82-0.91. Mechanism in the per-window rates: a DISCOUNT fill reaches the band in-match (W2) 86% and in the CORRIDOR 19%; a DISTORTION fill only 69% (W2) and 12% (corridor). W1 premarket-drift rarely reaches band for either (7%) - the fill is fresh, the band is above it. The dominant shape for BOTH is W2-only (63% of all legs): the band is reached in-match, per operator SS5 "only Window 2 is dictated by the game." Fill quality shifts the distribution: discount adds corridor-reachability and near-certain W2-reach; distortion pushes legs toward W2-only-OR-NONE. Sharpest consequence, on losers: a DISTORTION loser NEVER reaches the band (true knife) 43% of the time vs a DISCOUNT loser 19% - a bad fill roughly DOUBLES the rate of legs that ride to 0 with no exit window ever opening. Win-rate tracks fill quality directly: discount 56% vs distortion 41%.
